Cost of Living

This table contains various food, drink, and lodging related items/services. While these values are based upon the official 5e guidelines - which can be found on DM Screens for instance - I expanded them based on overall regional wealth as well as a short description/flavor. I also fixed a few mind-boggling math errors (how would a mug of ale be 1/5th the price of a gallon?) and converted from imperial to metric units where appropriate.

Item Cost Lifestyle Description
Drinks

Water

 1 cp Poor A simple mug of water, occasionally offered in poor taverns and inns. Tastes dirty.

Water

 1 gp Rich Clean water served in a luxurious cup. While not especially tasty, clean water serves as a status symbol in rich establishments in large and dirty cities.

Ale

 2 cp (mug), 6 cp (liter), 2.8 sp (barrel) Poor, Moderate Cheap ale sold en masse in low-income taverns and inns. It tastes more watery than most cups of water and serves as staple food for most peasants and workers.

Beer

 4 cp (mug), 1.2 sp (liter), 5.5 sp (barrel) Poor Strong beer traditionally brewed in monasteries and dedicated breweries. Its taste varies on multiple factors, such as its production (maybe roll a d20 to see how tasty it is).
